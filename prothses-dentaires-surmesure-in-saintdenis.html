Frequently Asked Questions About Prothèses dentaires sur-mesure

What is the typical cost range for dental prosthetics at your Saint-Denis clinic?
Our custom dental prosthetics pricing varies based on the specific type and complexity. As a general guide: partial removable dentures start from 500€, complete dentures from 900€, and dental bridges from 750€ per tooth. We accept most French mutual insurance plans (mutuelles) and offer payment plans in 3-4 installments. We provide detailed cost estimates during your initial consultation, which is covered by standard French social security.
How long does the entire process take from first consultation to receiving my custom prosthetics?
The timeline typically spans 3-4 weeks total. This includes: initial consultation (1 hour), impressions and measurements (1-2 visits), fabrication in our local laboratory (2-3 weeks), and final fitting appointment (1 hour). For immediate dentures following extractions, we can provide temporary prosthetics the same day, with permanent ones fitted after healing (approximately 6-8 weeks).
Do you offer emergency repairs for existing dental prosthetics?
Yes, we provide emergency repair services for dental prosthetics at our Saint-Denis clinic. Minor repairs can often be completed within 24 hours. We maintain extended hours Tuesday through Thursday until 19h00 specifically for emergency cases. For severe damage requiring complete replacement, we can typically provide temporary solutions while crafting your new prosthetics.
What guarantee do you offer on your custom dental prosthetics?
We provide a 2-year guarantee on all our custom prosthetics, covering manufacturing defects and fit issues. This includes free adjustments during the first 3 months to ensure optimal comfort. The guarantee requires following our care instructions and attending recommended check-ups every 6 months. Note that normal wear and tear or damage from accidents isn't covered under the warranty.
Can I get my prosthetics adjusted if they become uncomfortable after some time?
Yes, we offer adjustment services for both prosthetics made at our clinic and those made elsewhere. The first three adjustments within 3 months of receiving new prosthetics from us are free. After this period, or for prosthetics from other providers, adjustments cost 45-75€ depending on the complexity. We recommend immediate adjustments if you experience any discomfort to prevent oral tissue damage.
