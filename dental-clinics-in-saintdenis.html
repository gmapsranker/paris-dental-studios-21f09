Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Clinics

Do you accept CMU/AME and other French national health insurance coverage?
Yes, we accept CMU (Couverture Maladie Universelle), AME (Aide Médicale d'État), and all major French national health insurance coverage. We also work with most complementary health insurances (mutuelles). Please bring your Carte Vitale and attestation from your mutuelle during your first visit. We can help you understand what procedures are covered and provide cost estimates for any treatments not fully covered by insurance.
How long does it typically take to get an appointment for urgent dental care?
For dental emergencies (severe pain, trauma, or infection), we reserve several slots each day and typically can see you within 24-48 hours. For non-emergency cases, the average wait time for a first appointment is currently 1-2 weeks. You can call us at our Saint-Denis location or book online through our website. We're located near the Saint-Denis Basilique metro station for easy access.
What are your prices for basic dental procedures like cleanings and fillings?
A standard cleaning (détartrage) costs 28.92€, which is fully covered by Sécurité Sociale. For fillings, prices range from 30-75€ depending on size and material used. Composite (tooth-colored) fillings typically cost between 50-75€. Most basic procedures are covered at 70% by Sécurité Sociale, with the remainder often covered by your mutuelle. We can provide a detailed quote (devis) before beginning any treatment.
Do you offer orthodontic treatment for adults, and what payment plans are available?
Yes, we offer both traditional braces and clear aligners for adults. Treatment typically ranges from 18-24 months and costs between 2500-4500€ depending on complexity. We provide flexible payment plans with monthly installments starting from 100€/month. Initial orthodontic consultation is 40€ and includes a treatment plan with 3D imaging. Some mutuelles cover a portion of orthodontic treatment - we can help you check your coverage.
What languages do your dentists speak, and are you taking new patients?
Our dental team speaks French, English, and Arabic to serve our diverse Saint-Denis community. We are currently accepting new patients at our Marais location. Dr. Martin and Dr. Dubois are both certified by the Ordre National des Chirurgiens-Dentistes and have over 15 years of experience each. Your first consultation includes a complete oral examination, x-rays if needed, and a personalized treatment plan.
